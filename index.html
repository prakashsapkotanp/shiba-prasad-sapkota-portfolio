SHIBA PRASAD SAPKOTA, Ph.D.

Faculty of Management

Far Western University

Shiba Prasad Sapkota is an Associate Professor of Management at Kailali Multiple Campus, Far-Western University. He holds a Ph.D. in Finance and an M.Phil. in Finance and Econometrics, with research interests in public finance, fiscal governance, taxation, macroeconomic indicators, entrepreneurship, and accounting. He has published widely in national and international journals, authored academic books, and led research projects funded by UGC Nepal and provincial ministries.
